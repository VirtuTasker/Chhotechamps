Common Questions from Parents

What age group is Chhote Champs for? +
Our programs are designed for children aged 4 to 16 years. Each program is age-appropriate with separate curriculum tracks for early learners (4-7), middle childhood (8-11) and pre-teens (12-16).
How do I book a session? +
Simply tap any Book Session or Check Availability button on our website. A prefilled WhatsApp message opens — just send it, and our team responds within a few hours with slot options.
Are online classes as effective as offline? +
Yes! Our online sessions are 100% live and interactive — not pre-recorded. Many parents report equal or better results online, especially for shy children who are more comfortable at home.
Can my child do multiple programs together? +
Absolutely! Many families combine programs like Confidence Building plus Public Speaking, or Memory Training plus Focus Improvement. We help you plan the best combination for your child.
Are there any hidden fees or registration charges? +
None. What you see is what you pay. No registration fee, no materials charge, no hidden costs.
