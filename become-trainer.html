Trainer FAQs

Do I need prior experience with children? +
Prior experience is preferred but not mandatory for all roles. What matters most is your passion for children's development, your relevant qualifications and willingness to learn. We provide thorough onboarding and mentorship.
Can I work part-time? +
Yes! Many of our trainers work part-time — teaching 5 to 10 sessions per week alongside other professional commitments. You set your own schedule.
Can I train online from home? +
Absolutely. Online trainers work from home using Zoom or Google Meet. You need a good internet connection, a device with camera and a quiet, professional-looking space.
How are trainers paid? +
Payment details are shared during onboarding. Trainers are paid per session or per batch on a regular schedule via bank transfer or UPI.
Will I receive curriculum and session materials? +
Yes — all trainers receive comprehensive curriculum guides, activity materials, session plans and parent communication templates. You are never left without full support.
