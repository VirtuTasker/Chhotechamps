Pricing FAQs

Are there any registration or hidden fees? +
No. The prices shown are all-inclusive. There are zero registration fees, zero material fees and zero hidden charges — ever.
Can I switch programs after booking? +
Yes! You can change the program focus within your booked sessions at any time by informing your trainer. We are flexible.
Do you offer sibling discounts? +
Yes! We offer special family pricing for siblings. WhatsApp us to discuss a customised plan for your family.
What if my child misses a session? +
Missed sessions can be rescheduled with at least 4 hours advance notice. We understand that family schedules change.
How do I pay? +
Payment details are shared via WhatsApp after confirmation. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), bank transfer and all major payment methods.
Is pricing the same for all programs? +
Yes — the pricing above applies to all 20+ programs. No program costs more than another.
Can I combine programs in one pack? +
Yes! The 5-session and 10-session packs can be split across 2–3 different programs. Just tell us which combination you want.
