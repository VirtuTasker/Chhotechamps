General

What age group is Chhote Champs for? +
Our programs are designed for children aged 4 to 16 years. Each program has separate age-appropriate curriculum tracks for early learners (4–7), middle childhood (8–11) and pre-teens or teens (12–16). The trainer adapts activities and pace to each child's specific developmental stage.
What programs do you offer? +
We offer 20+ programs including Personality Development, Confidence Building, Public Speaking, Memory Training, Brain Development, Communication Skills, Focus Improvement, Handwriting Training, Creative Writing, Leadership Skills, Emotional Intelligence, Speed Reading, Yoga and Mindfulness, Art and Creativity, Storytelling, Time Management, Social Skills, STEM Thinking, English Speaking and Music and Rhythm.
How long is each session? +
Sessions are 45 to 60 minutes long. The trainer may extend slightly based on the activity flow and child's engagement. Online sessions follow the same duration.
How many sessions to see results? +
Most parents notice visible changes in their child's behaviour, confidence or skills within 3–5 sessions. Significant, lasting transformation typically occurs after a full 10-session program.
Can my child do multiple programs? +
Absolutely! Many children combine programs — for example, Confidence Building with Public Speaking, or Memory Training with Focus Improvement. Our team will recommend the best combination for your child's specific goals and age.

Online Classes

Are online classes as effective as offline? +
Yes! Our online sessions are 100% live and interactive — not pre-recorded. Trainers use real-time activities, games, exercises and feedback. Many parents report equal or better results online, especially for shy children who open up more easily in the comfort of their home environment.
What platform is used for online classes? +
We use Zoom or Google Meet for online sessions. The trainer shares the meeting link via WhatsApp before each session. No special software or subscription is needed.
Are session recordings provided? +
Yes. All online sessions are recorded and shared with parents within 24 hours via WhatsApp so your child can review key activities and learnings.
What cities can join online? +
Our online program is open to families anywhere in India — Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Kolkata and beyond. All you need is a stable internet connection and a device with a camera and microphone.
What time slots are available for online classes? +
We offer morning (7am–9am), afternoon (12pm–2pm) and evening (5pm–8pm) slots on weekdays, and multiple slots on weekends. Specific timing is confirmed during booking via WhatsApp.

Booking and Pricing

How do I book a session? +
Tap any Book Session, Check Availability or WhatsApp Now button on our website. A prefilled message opens in WhatsApp — just send it, and our team responds within a few hours with slot options tailored to your child's age, program and location preference.

Offline sessions start at Rs.999 per session, Rs.4,499 for a 5-session pack, and Rs.7,999 for a 10-session pack. Online programs start at Rs.499 per month, Rs.1,199 for 3 months, and Rs.1,999 for 6 months.
Are there registration fees? +
Absolutely not. There are zero registration fees, zero material fees and zero hidden charges. What you see on our pricing page is exactly what you pay.
How do I pay? +
Payment details are shared via WhatsApp after your booking is confirmed. We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), NEFT bank transfer and all major payment methods.
Do you offer sibling discounts? +
Yes! Families with multiple children receive special pricing. WhatsApp us to discuss a customised family plan.
What if my child misses a session? +
Missed sessions can be rescheduled with at least 4 hours advance notice. We understand that family schedules change and we try to be as flexible as possible.
Can I switch programs mid-way? +
Yes. If you feel a different program would benefit your child more, you can switch at any time. WhatsApp us and we will arrange the change seamlessly.

Trainers and Parents

How experienced are your trainers? +
All our trainers are certified professionals with a minimum of 3 years of experience working with children. They hold qualifications in psychology, education, NLP, child development or related fields and undergo regular training and quality reviews.
Will parents receive progress updates? +
Yes! After every session, the trainer sends a WhatsApp update summarising what was covered and how the child performed. Monthly progress reports provide detailed milestone tracking and recommendations.
Can parents observe sessions? +
We recommend parents do not observe sessions directly as children tend to behave differently when watched. For very young children under age 6, a parent may sit quietly in the background for the first session to help the child settle.
Is the trainer the same every session? +
Yes — we assign a consistent trainer to each child and maintain that relationship throughout your program. Consistency is important for building trust and tracking progress. Trainer changes are made only in exceptional circumstances.

Become a Trainer

How can I become a Chhote Champs trainer? +
Visit our Become a Trainer page and WhatsApp us your introduction and background. We are always looking for passionate, qualified trainers to join our growing team.
Do I need prior experience with children? +
Prior experience is preferred but not mandatory for all roles. What matters most is your passion for child development, your relevant qualifications and your willingness to learn. We provide comprehensive onboarding and mentorship.
Can I work part-time as a trainer? +
Yes! Many of our trainers work part-time — conducting 5 to 10 sessions per week. You choose your own schedule, making it ideal for educators, psychologists or anyone with relevant skills who wants flexible supplemental work.
