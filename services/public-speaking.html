About This Program

About Our Public Speaking Program

Public speaking is among the most valuable life skills a child can develop. Our program takes children from fear and hesitation to confident, compelling speakers through a progressive curriculum covering voice modulation, stage presence, storytelling, debate and impromptu speaking — preparing them to shine in any setting.
