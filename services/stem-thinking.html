About This Program

About Our STEM Thinking Program

STEM Thinking is about developing the mindset of scientists and engineers — curiosity, logical reasoning, hypothesis testing, creative problem-solving and comfort with uncertainty and failure. Our program uses hands-on experiments, puzzles and challenges to build these skills in children of all ages, regardless of their current interest in traditional academics.

No! STEM Thinking is about developing a mindset, not academic performance. Children with all levels of school performance benefit greatly.
What age is STEM training for? +
We work with children from age 6 with programs appropriate for different developmental stages up to age 16.
Is this available online? +
Yes! Most of our STEM activities use everyday household materials and are perfectly suited to online delivery.
How is this different from school science class? +
School science teaches content. Our program develops the scientific mindset, curiosity and problem-solving approach that makes great scientists.
