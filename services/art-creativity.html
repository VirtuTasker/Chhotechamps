FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need to be talented at art? +
Not at all! The program is about creative development, not talent. Many of our most transformed students started believing they could not draw at all.
What materials are needed? +
For offline, all materials are provided. For online, we send a simple materials list in advance — typically things already at home.
Is this available online? +
Yes! Online art sessions work beautifully with the trainer demonstrating and guiding through the camera.
How is this different from school art class? +
School art is curriculum-based. Our program is child-centred, focusing on creative thinking, individual expression and building creative confidence.
