About This Program

About Our Creative Writing Program

Creative writing is one of the most powerful forms of self-expression a child can develop. Our Creative Writing program nurtures each child's unique voice while teaching structure, vocabulary, grammar in context, storytelling techniques and the joy of crafting something original — building skills that enhance every area of their academic life.
