FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can confidence really be taught? +
Yes! Confidence is a skill, not a trait you're born with. With the right techniques and practice, any child can become genuinely confident.
My child is very shy — will group sessions overwhelm them? +
We start with one-on-one or small group settings and gradually increase exposure as the child grows comfortable.
How soon will I see results? +
Parents typically notice changes in 2–4 weeks. The child begins volunteering, speaking up and showing enthusiasm.
Is online confidence building effective? +
Yes! Trainers use interactive tools, games and exercises that work beautifully in virtual settings.
