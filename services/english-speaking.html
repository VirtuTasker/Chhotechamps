Frequently Asked Questions

My child understands English but will not speak it — will this help? +
This is exactly the profile our program is designed for. We create a safe, encouraging environment where hesitant speakers gradually build confidence.
What age is English speaking training for? +
We work with children from age 5 onwards. The earlier the better for natural fluency development.
Is this available online? +
Yes! Online English speaking sessions are highly effective — video calls require real English conversation.
How many sessions to become fluent? +
Fluency is a journey, not a destination. Most children show marked improvement within 6–8 sessions. A 10-session program creates a strong foundation.
