Parents typically notice improvement in homework completion and classroom behaviour within 4–6 sessions.
What techniques are used? +
Mindfulness, breathing exercises, sensory integration activities, timed focus challenges and positive reinforcement.

Yes — learning to focus during a video call is excellent practice for real-world concentration.
