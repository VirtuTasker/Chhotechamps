About This Program

About Our Handwriting Training Program

In an age of typing, beautiful and clear handwriting still matters enormously — for exams, impressions and the act of thinking itself. Our Handwriting Training program addresses grip issues, letter formation, spacing, cursive flow and writing speed through targeted exercises tailored to each child's specific challenges.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

At what age should handwriting training start? +
We work with children from age 4 (early printing) all the way to 16 (cursive refinement and speed). The earlier the better.
How many sessions to see improvement? +
Most children show clear improvement in handwriting quality within 4–6 sessions. Full transformation typically takes 8–12 sessions.
Is online handwriting training possible? +
Yes! The trainer guides exercises via video call while observing the child's writing technique through the camera. It works excellently.
Do you help with cursive specifically? +
Yes, cursive writing is a core part of our program. We teach the standard cursive script used in Indian schools.
