About This Program

About Our Emotional Intelligence Program

Research shows that Emotional Intelligence (EQ) predicts success in life more than IQ. Our EQ program teaches children to identify, understand and regulate their own emotions, develop empathy for others, manage conflict peacefully and build strong, healthy relationships — skills that benefit every area of life from age 4 onwards.
