Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from public speaking? +
Public speaking focuses on formal addresses. Communication skills covers all forms of interaction — conversations, listening, written expression and non-verbal cues.
Will this help at school? +
Better communication leads to clearer answers in class, improved writing and stronger peer relationships.
Is this available for very young children? +
Yes! Programs start from age 4, using games, puppets, stories and activities young children love.
How many sessions are needed? +
Most children show significant improvement in 6–8 sessions. A full 10-session program covers all core skills.
