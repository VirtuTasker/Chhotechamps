About This Program

About Our Music and Rhythm Program

Music and rhythm training offers profound cognitive benefits — from improved mathematical ability and memory to better language skills and emotional expression. Our program introduces children to the joy of music through rhythm exercises, basic music theory, singing, percussion and creative musical exploration — no prior musical experience needed.
How It Works

How Our Music and Rhythm Program Works

1

Musical Aptitude Assessment

2

3

4

Improvisation, composition and creative musical exploration building originality and joy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my child need any musical experience? +
No! Our program is designed for complete beginners. We start with the fundamentals in a fun, accessible way.
What instruments are used? +
We primarily use percussion instruments, voice and rhythm tools. No expensive instruments are required for our foundational program.
Is music training available online? +
Yes! Online music and rhythm sessions work very well — the trainer can hear and guide the child through the camera and microphone.
What age is music training for? +
We work with children from age 3. The earlier musical training begins, the more profound the cognitive benefits.
