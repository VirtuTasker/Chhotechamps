About Our Storytelling Program

Storytelling is the foundation of communication, creativity, public speaking and writing. Our Storytelling program teaches children the art of crafting and telling compelling stories — developing voice, character, plot, emotion and audience connection in a way that transforms how they communicate, write and present in every context.

Frequently Asked Questions

What age is storytelling training for? +
We work with children from age 4. Younger children focus on picture-based stories while older students develop complex narrative techniques.
Will this help with school presentations? +
Absolutely. Storytelling skills transfer directly to school presentations, public speaking and creative writing assignments.
Is online storytelling training available? +
Yes! Online storytelling sessions are vibrant and effective — the trainer and child exchange and perform stories via video call.
Does my child need to already like stories? +
A love of stories is great but not required. Many children discover a passion for storytelling through our program.
