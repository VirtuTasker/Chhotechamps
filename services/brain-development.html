Frequently Asked Questions

Can brain development really be trained? +
Absolutely. The brain is highly neuroplastic, especially in children. Targeted exercises create new neural pathways and strengthen existing ones.
What activities are used? +
We use puzzles, logic games, creative art, storytelling, spatial reasoning, pattern recognition and more — all designed by child development experts.
How is this different from tuition? +
Brain development training is about developing the cognitive capacity your child uses to learn any subject — not subject content itself.
Is the online program effective? +
Yes. Brain development exercises adapt well online with interactive digital tools and games.
